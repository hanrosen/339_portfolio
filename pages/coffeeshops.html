Coffeeshops

Below I have listed the 2 other coffeeshops in Ann Arbor where I like to do work. These two coffeeshops are Mighty Good Coffee and Espresso Royale. I tend to stay away from the big chains of Starbucks and Dunkin Donuts and prefer the smaller local chains.

Mighty Good Coffee

Mighty Good Coffee is a small coffeeshop I go to on South University Ave. Ever since sophomore year when I lived in a house on Hill St., I started looking for different coffeeshops that were closer to my house. Mighty Good Coffee was one of the first places I discovered and I still love to do work there. Here are some photos of the coffeeshop and its fresh coffee.

Espresso Royale

Espresso Royale is a college campus chain with two locations on opposite sides of the UM campus. Depending on whether I am going to work from home or from class, I will either go to the location on State St. or South University Ave. Here are some photos of Espresso and its food and coffee.

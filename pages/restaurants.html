Restaurants

Below I have listed my other favorite restaurant in Ann Arbor. I share some recommendations and photos of my favorite dishes. Also, because I have too many favorite restaurants, I have put a gallery below with pictures of dishes from other local restaurants.

Mani

Mani has been one of my favorite restaurants since I arrived in Ann Arbor almost 4 years ago. This Italian restaurant is known for their artisanal pizzas and moderately priced specialty foods.

Here is a photo of the bar and seating when you first enter the restaurant. I love the restaurant's vibe at night when the lights dim.

Here is Mani's famous caesar salad with an egg on top. A good caeser salad is one of my favorite foods and Mani's salad is on my list of top salads.

This is Mani's margarita pizza. While a classic, adding shredded parmesan cheese on top makes the pizza unique.

While this charcuterie board is a Mani special, I like to order it whenever available. The meats and cheeses are fresh and make for a great group appetizer.
